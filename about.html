Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate campers and nature lovers, Camping Curtea Larga was established with a simple mission: to create a place where people can reconnect with nature, relax, and create lasting memories. We poured our hearts into developing a campsite that balances comfort with the raw beauty of the outdoors.

What We Offer

  • Spacious Campsites: Whether you prefer pitching a tent under the stars or parking your RV in a scenic spot, we have a variety of spacious campsites to suit your needs.
  • Modern Amenities: Enjoy the best of both worlds with our clean and well-maintained facilities, including hot showers, restrooms, and a convenient camp store stocked with essentials.
  • Recreational Activities: From hiking and biking trails to fishing and boating, there's something for everyone at Camping Curtea Larga.
  • Family-Friendly Environment: Our campground is perfect for families, with playgrounds, picnic areas, and plenty of open space for kids to explore and play safely.
  • Pet-Friendly Policy: We know that pets are part of the family, so we welcome your furry friends to join you on your camping adventure.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Camping Curtea Larga, we are committed to preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. We implement eco-friendly practices such as recycling , sustainable waste management, and energy-efficient facilities to minimize our impact on the environment. Our goal is to ensure that future generations can enjoy the same breathtaking landscapes that our guests do today.
